US AI chip owners, end of 2025

Cumulative AI-chip installed base in GB300-equivalents (medians and 90% confidence intervals), per Epoch AI's Data on AI Chip Owners.

US owners only — Epoch also tracks a PRC aggregate and a global “Other” bucket, excluded here. Epoch publishes only a point estimate for xAI, so its bar has no interval. Counts are cumulative chips ever installed, with no depreciation or retirement adjustment. Hover or tap a bar for the chip-level breakdown.
